What you can actually do

Research

Map a market in minutes.

Competitor scans, regulatory landscapes, customer-segment research — Claude pulls, summarizes, and cites the sources you can defend in a meeting.

Write

First drafts that respect your voice.

Proposals, board updates, client emails, RFP responses. Give Claude two past examples and a brief — get a draft that doesn't sound like a robot.

Analyze

Upload a CSV of last quarter's pipeline, last month's expenses, last year's NPS. Ask plain questions. Get charts and the "so what" in one pass.

Organize

Twelve scattered vendor PDFs become one comparison table. Four meeting transcripts become a clean decision log with owners and dates.

Decide

Lay out a hard call — vendor choice, hire-or-promote, build-or-buy. Claude lists trade-offs, surfaces what you haven't considered, doesn't pretend to know your business better than you.

A pricing calculator. A meeting-prep checklist generator. A one-page client dashboard. Claude builds it as an Artifact — you use it five minutes later.
